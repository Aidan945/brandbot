A 3D robot mascot for your website.

Drop-in React component. It floats, gestures, blinks, and watches the visitor's cursor — and every color plus the chest logo are just props. No Spline runtime, no build step.

npm install brandbot

Drop it in. It just stands there.

In your app the robot sits locked in its box, faces forward, and follows the cursor. No dragging, no spinning off — exactly what you want in a hero or a card.

The showcase above adds orbit so you can spin it. Leave it off for production (the default).
